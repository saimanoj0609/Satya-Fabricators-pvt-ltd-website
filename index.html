What We Do

Manufacturing Capabilities

Comprehensive dished end manufacturing with world-class equipment and stringent quality controls.

Cold Pressing

High-capacity hydraulic presses from 150 Tons to 1200 Tons for cold pressing dished ends up to 90mm thickness and 5300mm span diameter.

Spinning

Imported Blue Valley (USA) Model 10 spinning machine with computerized facility for diameter up to 4200mm, 25mm MS and 20mm SS thickness.

Petal & Crown

Hemisphere dished ends up to 10,000mm diameter, and 2:1 ellipsoidal/torispherical upto 10,000mm with thickness up to 90mm.

Heat Treatment

Oil-fired furnace (4.3M W× 4.5M L× 2.0M H, 15 Ton capacity) approved by Lloyd's, IBR, EIL, and BVI for stress relieving and normalizing and Solution Annealing.

Codes & Certifications

Manufactured to international standards with inspection by globally recognized agencies.

ASME Sec VIII Div I & II

American Society of Mechanical Engineers — Pressure vessel design and fabrication code for unfired pressure vessels.

IBR

Indian Boiler Regulations — Mandatory compliance for boiler components including dished ends and headers.

BS 5500 & 1515

British Standards — Specification for unfired fusion welded pressure vessels and flanges.

IS 2825 & IS 4049

Indian Standards — Code for unfired pressure vessels and internal pressure design specifications.
