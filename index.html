What We Do

Manufacturing Capabilities

Comprehensive dished end manufacturing with world-class equipment and stringent quality controls.

Cold Pressing

High-capacity hydraulic presses from 150 Tons to 1200 Tons for cold pressing dished ends up to 100mm thickness and 5500mm span diameter.

Spinning

Imported Blue Valley (USA) Model 10 spinning machine with computerized facility for diameter up to 4200mm, 25mm MS and 20mm SS thickness.

Petal & Crown

Hemisphere dished ends up to 10,000mm diameter, and 2:1 ellipsoidal/torispherical upto 10,000mm with thickness up to 80mm.

Heat Treatment

Oil-fired furnace (4.2M × 4.5M × 2.0M, 15 Ton capacity) approved by Lloyd's, IBR, EIL, and BVI for stress relieving and normalizing and Solution Annealing.
