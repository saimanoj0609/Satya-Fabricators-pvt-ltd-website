What We Manufacture

Dished End Shapes & Types

We manufacture dished ends in various shapes and configurations — essential components for pressure vessels, heat exchangers, boilers, reactors, and storage tanks.

Hemisphere

Hemisphere Dished Ends

Hemispherical dished ends manufactured via cold pressing and crown & petal construction for large diameters. Ideal for high-pressure applications requiring uniform stress distribution.

Specifications

Maximum Diameter 15,000mm
Maximum Thickness 100mm
Construction Methods Cold Pressing, Crown & Petal
Semi-Ellipsoidal

2:1 Semi-Ellipsoidal Dished Ends

The most commonly used dished end type in pressure vessel construction. Offers an optimal balance between material economy and strength.

Specifications

Standard Diameter Up to 5500mm
Extended Diameter (Crown & Petal) 5500mm — 15,000mm
Max Thickness (CS) 100mm
Max Thickness (SS) 60mm
Knuckle Radius Up to 1500mm
Torispherical

Torispherical Dished Ends

Economical dished end design suitable for low to moderate pressure vessels. Available in standard and custom crown and knuckle radii.

Specifications

Standard Diameter Up to 5500mm
Extended (Crown & Petal) Up to 15,000mm
Maximum Thickness 100mm
