What We Manufacture

Dished End Shapes & Types

We manufacture dished ends in various shapes and configurations — essential components for pressure vessels, heat exchangers, boilers, reactors, and storage tanks.

Hemisphere

Hemisphere Dished Ends

Hemispherical dished ends manufactured via cold pressing and petal & crown construction for large diameters. Ideal for high-pressure applications requiring uniform stress distribution.

Specifications

Maximum Diameter 10,000mm
Maximum Thickness 90mm
Construction Methods Cold Pressing, Petal & Crown
Semi-Ellipsoidal

2:1 Semi-Ellipsoidal Dished Ends

The most commonly used dished end types.

Specifications

Standard Diameter Up to 5300mm
Extended Diameter (Crown & Petal) 5300mm — 10,000mm
Max Thickness (CS) 90mm
Max Thickness (SS) 60mm
Knuckle Radius Up to 1500mm
Torispherical

Torispherical Dished Ends

Economical dished end design suitable for low to moderate pressure vessels. Offered in standard and custom diameters, including petal & crown construction for larger sizes.

Specifications

Standard Diameter Up to 5300mm
Extended (Petal & Crown) Up to 10,000mm
Maximum Thickness 90mm
