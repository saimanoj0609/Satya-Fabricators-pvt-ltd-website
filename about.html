Company Profile

Precision Dished End Manufacturing

Satya Fabricators Pvt Ltd is a well-established manufacturer of Dished Ends, with high-capacity cold pressing ranging from 150 Tons to 1200 Tons and spinning of 25mm in Mild Steel and 20mm in Stainless Steel, with a maximum diameter of 4200mm.

The plant is managed professionally with qualified engineers on the shop floor. The Managing Director has an extensive track record in manufacturing chemical industrial plants and dished ends.

Centrally located in Hyderabad, our facility is well-connected by both road and rail transport for convenient logistics across India.
